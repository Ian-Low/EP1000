As with any machinery, the laser cutting machine doesn't stand out when it comes to safety. With the laser cutting machine, there are a lot of things to take note of before interacting with it.


1) Before starting, the user has to make sure the ventilation of fumes is open / turned on.
2) Ensure that Laser optics are cleaned regularly.
3) Ensure that user is being supervised at ALL times.
4) Adewuate gas and air flow.
5) Make sure to use materials that are allowed.
6) The user must know what to do in case of fires during operation. 7) Ensure the user knows where the Emergency Stop button and Fire Extinguihser are.
